LevaKost's Ugrumiy Weapon Pack — a Terraria content mod by LelaPo & Kotya.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*] Slime Shotgun & Slime Minigun — slime-based ranged weapons that leave puddles slowing enemies
[*] IBP-3000 — throwable explosive projectile with fire trail
[*] Fat Burger system — eat burgers to gain fat (defense & HP bonuses, but slower movement), use Diet Pills to slim down
[*] Spooky Candy — gambling consumable: 55% mega-heal, 30% explosion, 15% instant death with a joke
[*] Cherry — ultra-rare full HP restore with cooldown
[*] Cow NPC — a town "bull" that provides milk
[*] John NPC — rare cavern dweller; steal his hat and he'll fight you for it
[*] Cursed Bandle — AI-controlled accessory using Q-Learning that takes over your character
[/list]

Supports English and Russian localization.
